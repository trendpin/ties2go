About ties2go

Founded by a designer who got tired of boring boardrooms, ties2go is on a mission to bring joy, colour and personality back to menswear — without ever compromising on quality.

Every pattern starts as a hand-drawn illustration. Every tie is crafted with the same obsession as Savile Row tailoring. The result? Neckwear that makes you smile when you knot it.
